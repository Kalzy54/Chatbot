SECTION 1: LIBRARY OVERVIEW & MISSION
================================================================================

1.1 VISION STATEMENT
To be a modern and technology-driven university library that provides easy and 
timely access to knowledge and information, supporting teaching, learning, and 
research excellence at Mewar International University, Nigeria.

1.2 MISSION STATEMENT
Our mission is to enhance learning and research at Mewar International University, 
Nigeria by providing efficient access to library resources through a well-organized 
digital management system. We are committed to promoting academic growth, 
encouraging innovation, and ensuring that students, staff, and researchers can 
easily find and use information resources whenever they need them.

1.3 CORE VALUES
- Accessibility: Easy access to knowledge and information
- Innovation: Technology-driven services and systems
- Excellence: Supporting academic and research quality
- Integrity: Transparent and professional service delivery

SECTION 3: BORROWING POLICIES & LENDING SERVICES
================================================================================

3.1 LENDING POLICY - GENERAL INFORMATION
Loan Period: 7 days for all categories of users (students, staff, researchers)
Maximum Items: Varies by user category (consult library staff for details)
Renewal Policy: Renewal is allowed, subject to availability
Late Fees: Applied to overdue materials (rates available at circulation desk)
Lost/Damaged Items: User is responsible for replacement or payment

3.2 WHO CAN BORROW
- Current Students (undergraduate and postgraduate)
- Academic and Non-Academic Staff
- Registered Researchers
- Alumni (with proof of affiliation)
- External Borrowers (subject to verification and deposit)

3.3 HOW TO BORROW
1. Ensure you have a valid library card/student ID
2. Locate the book using the library catalog in ERP system
3. Bring the item to the circulation desk
4. Staff will scan your ID and the book
5. You will receive a receipt with due date

3.4 LOAN RENEWAL
- Renew before due date for extended borrowing
- Renewal allowed if item is not reserved by another user
- Renew online through library portal or in-person at circulation desk

SECTION 5: ACCESSING THE LIBRARY CATALOG
================================================================================

5.1 LIBRARY CATALOG & BOOK DISCOVERY
The Mewar University Library Catalog is integrated with the university ERP system

STEPS TO ACCESS:
1. Log in to the Mewar International University ERP Portal
2. Navigate to "Library" section in the main menu
3. Click on "Book List" icon
4. Enter search terms in the provided search space

5.2 SEARCH OPTIONS
Users can search and browse books by:
- Author Name (search by last name or full name)
- Book Title (search by exact title or keywords)
- Subject/Subject Area (browse by discipline or topic)

5.3 SEARCH TIPS
- Use author's last name for author searches
- Use keywords for broader title searches
- Subject search helps discover related materials
- Consult library staff for search assistance

SECTION 7: INSTITUTIONAL REPOSITORY
================================================================================

7.1 INSTITUTIONAL REPOSITORY OVERVIEW
Purpose: Preserve and provide access to university research outputs and 
intellectual contributions

7.2 REPOSITORY PLATFORM
Platform Software: DSpace (open-source digital library system)
Host Institution: Mewar International University
Content Types: Academic publications, research papers, theses, institutional 
documents

7.3 REPOSITORY MANAGEMENT PROCESS
PHASE 1: Establishment
- University academic director convenes academic meeting
- Solicit research articles and publications from all academic staff
- Formulate institutional repository policy
- Establish repository committee

PHASE 2: Staff Training
- Library staff training on DSpace platform operations
- Training on publication upload procedures
- Quality assurance and metadata standards
- Ongoing maintenance and access control

7.4 SUBMISSION PROCESS FOR RESEARCHERS
1. Register content with library staff
2. Provide publication details (title, author, date, abstract)
3. Submit digital file to designated staff member
4. Library staff reviews and uploads to DSpace
5. Content becomes publicly accessible

10.1 GENERAL QUESTIONS
Q: What is the loan period for books?
A: 7 days for all categories of users (students, staff, researchers)

Q: Can I renew my borrowed materials?
A: Yes, renewal is allowed subject to availability. Renew before the due date.

Q: What are the library hours?
A: Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Closed on weekends and holidays.

Q: How do I access the library catalog?
A: Log into the university ERP portal, go to Library section, click Book List 
   icon, and search by author, title, or subject.

Q: How do I contact the library?
A: Email: miulibrary2025@gmail.com or visit during operating hours.

10.2 ADVANCED QUESTIONS
Q: How do I request materials not in our catalog?
A: Use our Inter-Library Loan Service. Contact library staff for forms.

Q: How do I access digital resources from home?
A: Register for remote access. VPN credentials available from IT department.

Q: Can I submit my research to the institutional repository?
A: Yes, contact the library staff or Director of E-Library with your publication.

10.3 RESEARCH & ACADEMIC SUPPORT
Q: Who can help with my research project?
A: Ask a Librarian services (real-time or offline) provide research assistance.

Q: How do I learn about citations and bibliography?
