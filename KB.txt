ABOUT LIBRARY
Vision Statement
To be a modern and technology-driven university library that provides easy and timely access to knowledge and information, supporting teaching, learning, and research excellence at Mewar International University, Nigeria.

Mission Statement
Our mission is to enhance learning and research at Mewar International University, Nigeria by providing efficient access to library resources through a well-organized digital management system.We are committed to promoting academic growth, encouraging innovation, and ensuring that students, staff, and researchers can easily find and use information resources whenever they need them.
